How to use this:

  1. This site is to help anyone who is tracking childbirth labor contractions
  2. When a contraction starts hit the start button (or press a key like "s", enter/return, or the space bar). It will change to a "stop" button which...you should press again when the contraction stops.
  3. Work with your healthcare professional and do your own research to decide how to use this information, but...
  4. Common rules are "5-1-1" where you should wait for contractions to be 5 minutes apart each one lasting 1 minute over a 1 hour period before you go to the birthing center. This tool is oriented toward tracking a 5-1-1 scenario.
  5. You may also be in "serious labor" if you cannot talk through the contraction or are the contractions are intense/painful.
  6. Your data is only present inside of your browser and never sent to a server. If you refresh the page the data will disappear
  7. If you want to save the data, you can print the page or console.log(timerData); in the Javascript console. Or, you can work on the code to export data :)
